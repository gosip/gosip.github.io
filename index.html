I am a final-year PhD student at TCSLAB, Linköping University, Sweden supervised by Peter Jonsson and co-supervised by Victor Lagerkvist. Originally I'm from Georgia where I received a BSc in computer science at Free University of Tbilisi. I am part of AIICS division at IDA, Linköping University and also AI/math track of WASP graduate school.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science, with a focus on constraint satisfaction problems and parameterized complexity.
