About me

I am a PhD student at TCSLAB, Linköping University, Sweden supervised by Peter Jonsson and co-supervised by Victor Lagekvist. Originally I'm from Georgia where I received a BSc in computer science and studied towards a MSc in mathematics. I spent a year at Uppsala University thanks to the Erasmus+ exchange programme. I am part of AI/Math division of AIICS division at Linköping University and AI/math track of WASP graduate school.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science, with a focus on constraint satisfaction problems and parameterized complexity.
